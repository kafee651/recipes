#This document contain information about how to cook tea.

Material required:
1.Tea leaf.
2.Water.
3.Milk.
4.Sugar.

Procedure:
step1.Boil water with leaf while you find brown color.
step2.Mix sugar as per your test.
step3.Add milk as per your test.
step4.Wait for your tea to become deep brownies in color.
step5.Tea is ready to sirve
